Barbecues

Join us for a warm lunch.

SPS Barbecues are barbecue events that SPS hosts for the physics community roughly every month. In general they are held in the courtyard between Leconte and Birge on Fridays from noon to 2 pm. It's a great way for the physics community to mingle and meet new friends over good, affordable food. To make sure the barbecues run smoothly, roughly 3 to 5 volunteers help set up, cook, and/or clean up in exchange for free food. Barbecues are always a jolly good time for everyone.
