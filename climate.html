Department Climate

Taking care of our atmosphere.

There are a number of support communities within the Physics Department for various underrepresented groups. IGenSpectrum and the Society of Women in the Physical Sciences (SWPS) give voices to LGBT, female, and underrepresented students. The Compass Project aims to increase retention of students, particularly those who are part of underrepresented communities. All are awesome.

One fantastic resource on campus is the Gender Equity Resource Center (GenEq), a community of students dedicated to gender equality in our school.

If you believe you have been a victim of harassment, we encourage you to reach out to PATH to Care and the Office for the Prevention of Harassment and Discrimination.
