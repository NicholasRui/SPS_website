Problem of the Week

Problem: what is your purpose at Berkeley?

Problem of the weeks are problems that will be shown in the SPS website from Monday morning to Sunday night. It is meant to be done at your leisure time and done out of curiosity and not our of obligation. The winner will have a chance for their image and bio to be shown on this website.

If you think you have the answer and the steps to get there please email ucbsps “at” gmail “dot” com with the subject line “Problem of The Week. You can also email us if you have problem suggestions. The email must contain both the problem and the steps and the solution. It must be original, unique, and not easily searchable in the internet.
