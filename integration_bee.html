Integration Bee

May the best integrator win.

This year, on the evening of January 31, 16 brave integrators duked it out in the great battlefield that is Leconte 1. Hosted by Professor Paulin, and organized by SPS and MUSA, this was the inaugural integration bee in Berkeley.
